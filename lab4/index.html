Task 3

Sending important documents in the mail. 1.Figure out where you’re mailing this to. 2.Grab an empty, plain envelope. 3.Rotate the envelope so you’re looking at it horizontally. Hot dog style. 4.In the top left corner, write your address. For example: Sammy Slug 1156 High St Santa Cruz, CA 95064 5.In the center, write the recipient’s address formatted similarly to yours. 6.Place documents inside and seal. 7.Place the appropriate postage in the top right corner. 8.Drop off in the nearest Post Office Box. 9.Celebrate when the recipient gets it!
